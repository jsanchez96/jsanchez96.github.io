Milwaukee County Separate and Unequal

This map is a bivariate choropleth representation that compares location of Milwaukee County's poor populations and distribution of minority populations based on 2010 census data. I used the parameters of all populations living below the poverty line and all people who identified as non-white and/or hispanic, all at the census tract level. I grew up in Milwaukee and will always consider it to be home; I wanted to highlight that much of the disparity in the area is both tied to race and poverty and concentrated in the city compared to the suburbs.
