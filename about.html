Who am I?

I am Jacob Sanchez, cartographer, geographer and enjoyer of all things beautiful. I am currently in my third year as an undergrad at the University of Wisconsin-Madison studying People and the Environment Geography and Cartography/GIS. Beyond my studies I have a passion for cycling that takes me to new and exciting places both near and far.

As an employee of the UW Cartography Lab I have had the opportunity to work with clients on a variety of projects including a comprehensive world map and editing the official UW campus print map. My hope is to some day combine my passion for all things outdoors with my love for all things map related to better both the communities that I call home and those across the globe. I am always looking for new opportunities to challenge myself in cartographic design and the field of geography.
